Quick Start Guide
• To record your active window with audio, click 'Record'.
• Right click the flashing icon in system tray to 'Stop/Pause'.
• Adjust microphone sound settings using 'Hardware' tab.
 
Hot Keys
• Record/Resume = Win-Alt-R.
• Pause = Win-Alt-P.
• Stop = Win-Alt-S.
 
Click 'Help' to view our forum or check for updates.
